## gDNA prep — 8 transformants — 2026-05-12

Pulling genomic DNA from all 8 patched transformants for the DemoCheck PCR screen tomorrow.

### Reagents (per sample)

- Breaking buffer (2% Triton X-100, 1% SDS, 100 mM NaCl, 10 mM Tris pH 8, 1 mM EDTA): 200 µL
- Acid-washed glass beads (0.5 mm): ~200 µL bed volume
- Phenol:chloroform:IAA (25:24:1): 200 µL
- Ethanol (100%, ice-cold): 1 mL
- TE buffer: 50 µL

### Steps (Hoffman-Winston, condensed)

1. Pellet 1.5 mL overnight, decant.
2. Resuspend in 200 µL breaking buffer + beads + 200 µL phenol mix.
3. Vortex 3 min, top speed.
4. Spin 13k rpm × 5 min. Take ~150 µL upper phase.
5. Add 1 mL EtOH, mix, spin 13k rpm × 2 min.
6. Wash pellet with 500 µL 70% EtOH, spin 2 min, decant, air-dry.
7. Resuspend in 50 µL TE.
