# [Demo protocol] Yeast transformation (LiAc)

Lithium acetate transformation, scaled for one 50 mL culture and ten reactions.

## Reagents

- 50% PEG-3350 (sterile)
- 1 M LiAc (sterile)
- Salmon-sperm DNA carrier, 10 mg/mL
- TE buffer
- SD selection plates (SD-Ura for `pYES-GAL1::flbA`, SD-His for `pDEMO-fluo`)

## Steps

1. Grow `FakeYeast-001` overnight in 5 mL YPD at 30 °C.
2. Dilute into 50 mL fresh YPD, OD600 ~0.2. Grow to OD600 ~0.8 (~4 h).
3. Spin 3 min at 3,000 g. Wash pellet with 25 mL sterile water, then 1 mL 100 mM LiAc.
4. Resuspend in 1 mL 100 mM LiAc → 50 µL aliquots.
5. Per reaction: 240 µL PEG, 36 µL 1 M LiAc, 50 µL boiled carrier DNA, 1–5 µL plasmid (~100 ng).
6. Vortex hard, heat-shock 42 °C, 40 min.
7. Spin gently, resuspend in 200 µL water, plate on SD selection.

## Common gotchas

- Skip the heat-shock step if the strain is heat-sensitive (e.g., `DemoStrain ΔADE2`). Substitute 30 °C, 90 min.
- Always run a no-DNA control on the same SD plate.
