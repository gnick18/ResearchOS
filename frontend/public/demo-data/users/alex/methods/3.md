# [Demo protocol] Plasmid mini-prep

Quick alkaline-lysis mini-prep tuned for the lab's plate-format work.

## Reagents

- Resuspension buffer (P1) with RNase A
- Lysis buffer (P2): 0.2 M NaOH, 1% SDS
- Neutralization buffer (P3): 3 M KOAc pH 5.5
- 70% ethanol, ice cold

## Steps

1. Spin 1.5 mL overnight culture at 13,000 rpm, 1 min. Decant.
2. Resuspend pellet in 250 µL P1, vortex.
3. Add 250 µL P2, invert 6×, incubate 3 min RT.
4. Add 350 µL P3, invert 6×, spin 10 min at 13,000 rpm.
5. Transfer supernatant to spin column, wash with 70% EtOH, elute in 50 µL TE.

Expected yield for the demo strain library: 80–150 ng/µL.
