# [Demo protocol] Heat-shock survival assay

Plate-based survival measurement after a sub-lethal heat shock.

## Steps

1. Grow strains to OD600 ~0.6 in YPD.
2. Split each culture into two: one at 30 °C (control), one at 50 °C (heat shock), 30 min.
3. Serial dilute 10-fold five times.
4. Spot 5 µL of each dilution on YPD plates. Two technical replicates per condition.
5. Grow 48 h at 30 °C. Photograph plates; count colonies at the dilution with 10–100 colonies.

Survival = (CFU heat shock) / (CFU control) × 100%.
