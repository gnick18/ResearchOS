BeakerBot Demo Protocol — Coffee Brewing v0.1

Step 1: Heat 250 mL of filtered water to 96 C.
Step 2: Grind 15 g of medium-roast beans to medium-coarse.
Step 3: Bloom with 30 mL of water for 30 seconds.
Step 4: Pour the remaining water in two even pulses.
Step 5: Total brew time should be 3 to 4 minutes.
