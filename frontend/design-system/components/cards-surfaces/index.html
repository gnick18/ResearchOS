Cards and surfaces

ResearchOS uses three stacked surface tokens to signal depth. A page surface holds raised cards, and each card can hold a sunken inset. The shadow only ever lives on the raised card.

Read order. Lower contrast reads as further back. --surface is the matte canvas, --surface-raised floats on it with the card shadow, and --surface-sunken recesses again for inputs, metadata, and inset wells. Stacking more than three layers flattens the hierarchy, so nest no deeper than this.
