Your research stays on your computer.

ResearchOS is local-first. You pick a folder, and every experiment, note, and measurement lives inside it, right there on your machine. The browser reads and writes that folder directly.

  • Your folder never uploads. There's no ResearchOS server reading it.
  • We can't see your data. The website only sees what your browser puts on screen.
  • One anonymous pageview ping, and you can turn it off in Settings.

So what does touch the cloud, and when? That's the next screen, and it's worth a minute.

This is the onboarding we are taking to a 10 out of 10. The five beats are Welcome, Where your work lives, the animated Data flow explainer, Why this is cheap and private, then Set up your folder. The Skip link in the corner is always an escape, since the whole walkthrough is opt-in.
