BeakerBot speech bubble

The intermittent bubble from frontend/src/components/beakerbot/BeakerSpeech.tsx. Each line types in with a blinking caret, holds, fades, then a long silent gap. The bubble is max-content width capped at ~18rem, white with a sky-300 border, and a two-triangle CSS-border notch points at the mascot.

The mascot is always BeakerBot. Lines read in his voice typeface (Hanken Grotesk, the same as his AI-chat replies) so the bubble sounds like BeakerBot talking, not UI chrome. Clicking a visible bubble skips to the next line.
