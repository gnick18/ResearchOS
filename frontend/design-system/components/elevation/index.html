Elevation and shadow

The raised-shadow aesthetic is locked. Three tiers, each a soft contact shadow plus a long ambient cast, so a surface reads as lifted off the page rather than outlined. Depth climbs from popover to card to button-on-press.

Popover
Popover shadow
--ros-popover-shadow
Lightest lift. Dropdowns, menus, and popovers that float just above their trigger.
Card
Popup card shadow
--ros-popup-card-shadow
Deepest cast. Modals and cards that sit clearly above the whole page.
Button raise
--ros-btn-raise
Tactile lift on every solid button. Deepens to ros-btn-raise-hover on hover.
