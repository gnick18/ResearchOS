Marketing backdrop

The shared ambient stage from frontend/src/components/marketing/MarketingBackdrop. Four soft pastel-rainbow auroras drift slowly over a faint masked dot grid, the same brand wash the welcome, pricing, and walkthrough surfaces sit on. Decorative only, behind the content, and the drift stops under reduced motion. Shown at the vivid tone.

One continuous stage

Content floats above the wash on a relative z-10 sibling, so every marketing page reads as one place instead of three unrelated white pages.

The auroras share the BeakerBot liquid ramp (peach, yellow, mint, sky, lavender), the same stops as the banner and avatars. Drift translates only (never scales) so the 64px blur rasterizes once and rides the compositor.
