Radius and spacing

Four corner radii cover the whole surface vocabulary. Radius scales with the size of the thing, so a chip and a large popup never share a curve.

6
Small
--radius-sm, 6px
8
Medium
--radius-md, 8px
12
Large
--radius-lg, 12px
16
Extra large
--radius-xl, 16px

Where each goes. sm for inputs and chips, md for buttons, lg for cards, xl for large popups.
