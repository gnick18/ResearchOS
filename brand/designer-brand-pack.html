Hey! Meet BeakerBot

Hey! Here's everything to play with. The whole idea: redraw BeakerBot (or the logo) in whatever cool, weird, fun art style you want. Truly zero rules, the only soft ask is that he stays a friendly little beaker with a face and pastel-rainbow goo inside. Past that, go nuts. Editable vector files are in the assets/ folder next to this page.

That's him: a beaker, two eyes, a smile, a tiny antenna, pastel rainbow liquid. He can wear stuff, hold stuff, be any style. The liquid just stays soft pastel (not the punchy logo rainbow). That's basically the only "rule".

Font & logo

Brand font is Geist (free, on Google Fonts). The wordmark: "Research" in the surface color, "OS" in the rainbow. Vivid OS on light, luminous OS on dark. SVG + PNG in assets/logo/.
