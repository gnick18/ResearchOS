Aurora blur performance: scale (old) vs translate (fix)

Open this in a NORMAL visible tab (rAF is suspended in background tabs). It builds a stack of the real aurora layers (560px, blur 64px) and measures frame rate while they animate. "Scale" reproduces the old keyframe (animates transform scale on a blur, which re-rasterizes every frame). "Translate" is the shipped fix. Higher layer count = more stress; bump it until you see a gap.
