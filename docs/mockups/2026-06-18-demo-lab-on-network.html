The Castellanos Lab

We engineer FakeYeast strains to push metabolite yields past what wild strains can reach. We publish our methods, our strain builds, and our analysis so other labs can reproduce and build on them.

What we work on

  • High-yield metabolite pathways in FakeYeast
  • Reproducible strain-engineering protocols
  • Open datasets and figures, shareable in one step

Engineering FakeYeast for high-yield metabolites

Castellanos M, Mercado A, Lee M. FakeYeast Journal, 2026 (fabricated).

Abstract

We report a reproducible pipeline for engineering FakeYeast strains that raises target metabolite yield by a factor of three over the parent strain. All strains, protocols, and raw measurements are published openly.
