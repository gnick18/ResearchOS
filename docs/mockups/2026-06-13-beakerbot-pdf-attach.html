After the user approves a card, BeakerBot confirms the write and the card collapses into a compact "Written" confirmation. After rejection the card dims to a "Skipped" state. Neither writes anything without explicit approval. The source passage above the preview is the guard against hallucination: the user can check the quoted text against the open PDF before approving.
