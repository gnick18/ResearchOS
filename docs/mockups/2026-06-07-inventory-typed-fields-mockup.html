When an item's category is Antibody or Plasmid, the item form grows a small typed-fields section, and those fields show on the row. Same stock/signal/location backbone, only extra descriptive fields per type. Antibody + Plasmid first (the design's first two registries); enzyme, primer, cell line follow later. Fields are all optional. Sample data.
