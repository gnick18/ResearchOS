What it does

  1. Pinned graphic map. The signature visual stays at the top: a PCR thermocycler profile (temp/time blocks + the cycle bracket) or an LC gradient curve. The current phase is highlighted on it as you advance.
  2. Hybrid steps. Big-text steps scroll; the focused one enlarges. Reagents become a big checklist, temps render huge (44px) and color-coded.
  3. Next / Prev advance the focused step and re-center; tap any step to focus it. Progress dots at the bottom.
  4. Both entry points. A new Method tab (badged), plus the Read-mode button to go full-screen big-text.
  5. Screen stays awake (expo-keep-awake) and Add-variation is one tap, without leaving the flow.
