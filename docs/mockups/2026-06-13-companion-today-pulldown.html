Companion Today, pull-down redesign

Drag the handle at the top of the phone down (or tap it) to reveal Today. Set verdicts, pin notes, Export.

Today moves off the Notebook body and becomes an Apple-style panel you pull down from the top of the screen. The capture actions go back to the top (no scrolling past Today, which also fixes the demo clip). The summary is revamped into three glanceable stat tiles (Today / Overdue / Coming up) over contained task rows, so you sum up the day in one glance and one gesture. Try it: drag the grab handle at the very top of the phone downward, or tap it. Dismiss by dragging the bottom handle up, tapping the dimmed area, or the handle.

What changed

  1. Today leaves the Notebook. The capture actions (Take a photo, Quick note, Scan) sit at the very top now, nothing to scroll past.
  2. Pull down from the top to summon Today, like the iOS Notification Center / Today view. A slim grab handle at the top is the affordance.
  3. One-glance summary. Three stat tiles count Today, Overdue (red), and Coming up (amber) before any list, so you sum the day instantly.
  4. Contained rows. Compact, color-ticked rows grouped under the tiles, not full-height cards.
  5. Dismiss by dragging the bottom handle up, tapping the dimmed area, or the handle.
