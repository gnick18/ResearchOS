Send destination picker

When the phone sends a capture, route it to where you are working on the laptop. Inbox, experiment tab, or a specific note entry.
ApproveChange?

The phone reads the laptop focus context (what is pulled up right now) just before a capture sends, then offers the right destination. Three cases: nothing open goes straight to the inbox (no prompt), an open experiment asks Lab Notes vs Results, and an open note offers its entries. For a multi-entry running note the picker leads with a recommended entry (the one open on the laptop, else the last-edited) and lists all entries beneath it. The laptop does the actual write (it owns the data folder) and auto-switches to where the item landed. "Send to inbox instead" is always one tap away.
