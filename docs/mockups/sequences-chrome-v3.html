What this is showing

  • Three rows become two zones. A constant top action bar and a constant bottom view bar. The canvas grows; the chrome stops stacking.
  • Nothing jumps. Switching tabs or flipping topology never adds or removes a row. On Features / Primers / History the Show chips and zoom simply disable (they stay in place, greyed). The Circular / Linear chip relabels in the same slot, and Wrapped disables on a ring rather than vanishing.
  • View tabs are pinned at the very bottom and stay constant across every mode (the SnapGene spine), with Find on the left and the live coordinate + organism readout on the right.
  • Edit dropdown removed. Copy (with its full family), Cut, Paste stay as buttons. Select All / Range / Invert, Make Upper / Lower, and Go To live in the right-click menu and the keyboard; Find gets a dedicated control on the bottom bar.
  • The right operations rail + inspector (the Phase 1 work) are unchanged.

Before: action bar + view tabs + Show strip stacked at the top (3 rows), with the Show strip and topology chips appearing, disappearing, and relabeling per mode. After: 1 top row, 1 bottom zone, both fixed.
