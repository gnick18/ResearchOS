What changed from v3

  • Editor title row removed. The editor now starts at the action bar, reclaiming a full row of vertical space for the canvas.
  • The left bar is contextual. Its header reflects the open sequence, name, type, topology, length, feature count, and organism, so the sidebar tells you what you are working on instead of just listing files. With nothing open it falls back to the generic "Sequences" intro.
  • Share + Focus moved to the sidebar header (the two actions that used to sit in the title row). Focus mode collapses the sidebar to give the canvas full width, with a thin handle on the left edge to bring it back.
  • Open different sequences below to watch the header (and the list highlight) update. Linear vs circular is reflected in the header meta and the molecule glyph.
  • Everything else is v3: the constant top action bar (no Edit) and the constant bottom view bar (Show + zoom, then the pinned tabs).
