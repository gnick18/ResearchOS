What a department includes →

A department costs less per lab than a standalone lab, on purpose. Landing a whole department brings many labs at once, so we reward it instead of taxing it.

Prices show per month, but we bill off a running balance. Your card only runs once you owe about $5, or when you close, so it is a couple of charges a year, not a monthly bill.

Joining a lab your PI runs?

That is free, not a paid plan. Accept the invite your lab head sent you. Only the lab head pays, members never do, so do not start a new lab.

Sequencing keeps your locked "free wizard first" intent: a brand-new visitor can still hit "Start free" and explore with no login. This Free-vs-Paid screen is the choice moment, and "Start free" still leads to the account pitch (1.6M token gift + AI-tailored onboarding) or staying local.
