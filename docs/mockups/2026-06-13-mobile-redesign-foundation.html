Companion UI — Redesign Foundation

The design contract foundation. This locks the navigation model, the design language (color, type, spacing, depth, motion), the component library, and two hero screens. Once approved, every remaining screen and popup gets built to this. Research-backed: ≤5 destinations, capability tabs, actions off-tab, 8pt grid, oversized bench targets, queue-and-sync offline.

01 · Navigation & hero screens

Four capability tabs, a center Capture action, anchored by Home

Six tabs exceeded the ~5 ceiling (Apple HIG, NN/g, Material). New model: Home · Notebook · ＋Capture · Methods · Inventory. Timers, Calculators, Wiki and Settings move into the Home hub and header. Tap the bottom bar on the first phone to switch screens.

Why this model. Tabs now name what the companion is (capture, reference, inventory) instead of dumping every feature. The center ＋ is the signature bench action and is reachable from any screen. Home is the glance surface, your live timers, today, sync state, and a launcher for the rest. Nothing lands in a discoverability-killing "More" bucket.
Where the old tabs went. Notebook → Notebook. Method → Methods. Inventory → Inventory. Calc + Timers + Wiki → Home hub launcher (timers also glance live on Home). Notifications + Settings → header icons. Capture actions (photo, note, scan) → the center ＋ sheet.
Bench-tuned. Primary actions are oversized well past the 44pt minimum for gloved, one-handed, in-motion use. Connection state always shows icon + label + color, never color alone. Offline captures queue and sync, never block.
02 · Design language

Color, type, spacing, depth — one token scale, no one-off values

The single highest-leverage "looks designed" lever (Refactoring UI, Material): discrete predefined scales for everything. Brand sky stays; the rest is refined for depth and restraint.
