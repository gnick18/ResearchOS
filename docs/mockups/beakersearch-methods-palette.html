BeakerSearch on the Methods page, before and after

Empty-query Cmd-K with one of your methods open in the library. Mark each section below.

Before (today)

The Methods page registers no source, so Cmd-K shows only the always-on global layer. No library context, no method actions, no template browsing, no jump-to.

After (proposed Methods source)

A context card (library scope + the open method), permission-aware actions (edit / rename / move / fork / share / delete), and the page's two navigable kinds, jump to YOUR methods (Open) and the catalog TEMPLATES (Use).

What the Methods source adds. Mark each Approve or Disapprove.

All actions wire to the page's real handlers (per docs/proposals/beakersearch-methods.md). Writes gate on useMethodPermissions().canModifyMethod, shared-with-edit routes through ownerScopedMethodsApi, fork is allowed even on read-only methods. Methods reuse the existing emerald chip; catalog templates stay neutral and are told apart by a library icon + the "Use" hint (no new color). Approve, or say color templates.
