Join the Department of Microbiology

Dr. Vega (Microbiology, UW–Madison) invited your lab to be sponsored by the department.

  • The department pays for your lab’s cloud storage on its invoice — you and your members stop being billed individually.
  • You keep full control of your data and your members. The department admin never sees your notes, experiments, or files.
  • They can see your account names and storage/activity totals (so they can size the shared plan), and that your lab is in the department.
  • You can leave the department anytime — your lab reverts to billing on its own, nothing is deleted.
