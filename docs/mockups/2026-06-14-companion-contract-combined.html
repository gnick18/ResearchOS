At the bench

Capturing photos and notes

Updated Jun 10 · 3 min read

The Notebook tab is your inbox. Anything you capture queues there and syncs to your laptop automatically when you are online.

Take a photo

Tap the center ＋ and choose Take a photo. Add a caption, optionally annotate, then send it to your inbox.

Work offline

No signal at the bench is fine. Captures wait in the inbox marked "Waiting for connection" and send themselves the moment you reconnect.

Filing happens on send. Pick a notebook, or leave it unsorted for the laptop.
Wiki page reader
Shared reader styling with the markdown method type. Search in-page from the header.
