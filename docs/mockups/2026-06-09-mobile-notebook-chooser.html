Capture a note, pick ANY notebook to file it in (yours, shared, 1:1), or leave it unsorted.

The reframe. Instead of only routing to whatever is open on the laptop, the phone shows a chooser of every notebook you can write to, so the companion is a real capture tool, not just a remote. The chooser leads with a recommended row (what is open on your laptop, if anything), then your own notebooks, notebooks shared with you (edit access only, view-only ones are shown locked), and your 1:1 notebooks with your PI or students. "Unsorted note" always sits at the bottom for a quick capture that is not filed (it lands in the inbox). Picking a multi-entry running notebook then asks which entry (the recommended-entry picker we already mocked). The laptop does the write, with the edit-permission gate and correct attribution.
