Project surface redesign — full-page route → focused popup

What is a project surface FOR?

The test: what can a user do here that they can't do anywhere else? Today this surface is a full-page route with six tabs, and a third of its top actions (Edit, Archive, Delete) are already on the project card's kebab menu. The genuinely unique value is the project's identity and its launchpad.

Proposed purpose. The project popup is the project's home base. It answers "what is this, how's it going, and where do I go next." It owns only what is project-level-unique (the About overview, funding, share, deposit, version history) plus a compact status glance, and it launches into the heavy views (Gantt, results, methods) rather than embedding all of them. It opens over the Workbench so you never navigate away.
