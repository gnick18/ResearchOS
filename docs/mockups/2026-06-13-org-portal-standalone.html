First sign-in provisions your admin identity. A resettable shared access code (the everyday door) + one root recovery account come with the full portal-login layer, designed, not yet built.

No "create a folder" gate, no app nav. A logged-out visitor lands here, not on the 401 create-form you hit earlier.

Entry point. A "For departments & institutions" band on the welcome page links straight into each portal. Researchers self-serve into the app; org admins click through to the standalone portal.
