How it works#security
Your desktop creates a short-lived pairing code, signed with your ResearchOS identity key (the same Ed25519 key the sharing/collab features already use). The QR carries that signed grant plus where to sync.
Your phone scans it and generates its OWN device key, which never leaves the phone (stored in the iOS keychain via the secure enclave).
The phone registers its device public key with the capture relay (the small Cloudflare Worker), authorized by the signed grant. The relay now trusts this device as yours.
From then on the phone signs each capture upload with its device key. The relay verifies the device is registered to you before accepting. You can unpair a device anytime from the desktop, which revokes it.
__LOCK__ Private keys never leave their device. The QR grant is short-lived (a couple of minutes). The relay only ever holds captures in transit to your folder, never your private notes.

Note: this depends on the capture relay (the small Worker from the build-plan D2 decision), the phone registers with it during pairing. So Chunk 1 and that Worker get built together.
