What Phase 7 adds

Embeds today are always live, which is right most of the time and is why collaboration and version control work for free. Phase 7 is the finishing layer. Freeze a figure as a record of a day and know when its source moves on, reuse a protocol paragraph everywhere, flip how an embed is shown without editing markup, cite a paper or a compound you have not saved, and make all of it read correctly on a phone. The raw stays a portable link throughout, so nothing here breaks the contract.

Decisions, mark up each

Each fork has a recommended option (green). Approve to take the recommendation, Modify with a note, or Reject to defer. I build exactly what you approve, in the recommended order below. FLAG marks a data-shape touch I hold for your verify.

Recommended build order
  1. P7-3 view switching. Pure UI over the renderers, no data-shape, a fast win.
  2. P7-1 pinning and staleness. Introduces the <note>.ros-embeds.json sidecar that P7-4 reuses.
  3. P7-2 transclusion. Builds on the parser and depth guard already in place.
  4. P7-4 external embeds. Reuses the literature companion fetch path and the P7-1 sidecar cache.
  5. P7-6 mobile and accessibility. A finishing pass once the renderers are final.
  6. P7-5 BeakerBot authoring. Relayed to the BeakerAI lane, ships on the AI timeline.
