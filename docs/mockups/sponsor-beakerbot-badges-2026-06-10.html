Design rationale

The escalation at a glance

Each tier step adds one visible signal through posture and motion. BeakerBot's color stays sky-blue on every tier. No accessories, no crowns, no color changes.

Backer ($5/mo)
Idle and alive: calm, gentle sway, blink, gaze drift. Arms at rest, small smile, standard rainbow liquid. The minimum readable form of the character. Sky-blue throughout.
Patron ($15/mo) -- featured
Double-wave: both arms raised up in the air, waving continuously. The two arms are out of phase so they wave alternately. Visibly enthusiastic. Still sky-blue, no accessories.
Benefactor ($25/mo)
Twirl: a continuous 360deg rotation, gently spinning with confetti scattered around. Confetti uses the same pastel rainbow palette as the liquid. High excitement, unmistakably celebrating. Sky-blue, no crown.
Decisions reflected in this mockup
  • Tier names are Backer / Patron / Benefactor, matching the live /thanks page TIERS array exactly.
  • Prices are $5 / $15 / $25 per month. Patron is the featured center tier ("Most chosen").
  • BeakerBot stays sky-blue on every tier. Escalation is pose and motion only: idle/alive, double-wave, twirl. No color changes, no crowns, no gold.
  • Confetti on Benefactor uses only the pastel rainbow palette (sky, mint, lavender, peach, pink). Matches the ConfettiLayer the real /thanks page renders for that tier.
  • The 100x100px PNG exports for GitHub Sponsors use a transparent background so the badge works on both GitHub's light and dark chrome.
  • SPONSORS.md lives at the repo root, as referenced in the Patron perk text.
