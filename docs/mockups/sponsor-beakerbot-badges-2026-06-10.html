Design rationale

The escalation at a glance

Each tier step adds one visible signal so the badge reads immediately without a label, while all three are unmistakably the same BeakerBot.

Bench
Plain BeakerBot. Standard liquid, calm eyes, small closed smile. Sky-blue stroke, no accessories. The minimum readable form of the character.
Lab
Raised eyebrows, wider grin, slightly larger eyes, richer liquid saturation. One gold sparkle cross floating beside the head. Visibly happier, not yet over the top.
Institute
Gold crown with three points and gem accents. High brows, fully open celebration mouth (filled arc). Confetti scattered around. Gold-tinted liquid. The full celebration read, clearly distinct at any size.
