RNA Extraction, TRIzol Method
6 steps
1
Harvest cells and add TRIzol reagent
Lyse directly in the culture dish or collect pellet first
1 mL per 10 cm dish 5 min incubation
2
Add chloroform and mix vigorously
Shake tube by hand for 15 s; do not vortex
200 µL chloroform 2 min RT
3
Centrifuge to separate phases
Upper aqueous layer contains RNA
4 °C 12,000 g 15 min
4
Transfer aqueous phase; precipitate RNA with isopropanol
500 µL isopropanol 10 min on ice
5
Pellet RNA; wash with 75% ethanol
4 °C 7,500 g 5 min 75% EtOH
6
Air-dry pellet; resuspend in RNase-free water
Proceed to cDNA synthesis or store at -80 °C
30 µL nuclease-free H2O -80 °C storage
5 of 6 steps completed
